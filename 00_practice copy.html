Sample Problem 1

Which statement correctly describes chemistry as the central science?

A. Chemistry only studies synthetic materials.
B. Chemistry connects physics at the atomic scale with biology at the living-systems level, making it relevant to all sciences.
C. Chemistry is a branch of physics with no connection to biology.
D. Biology is more central because it studies all living things.
Answer: B
Chemistry is called the central science because it links physical science and life science.

Sample Problem 2

When reading volume in a graduated cylinder, you should read from the:

A. Top of the meniscus from above
B. Bottom of the meniscus at eye level
C. Middle of the meniscus from any angle
D. Side of the cylinder at any height
Answer: B
For water and most aqueous solutions, read the bottom of the meniscus at eye level.

Sample Problem 3

Convert 2.75 L to milliliters.

A. 0.00275 mL
B. 27.5 mL
C. 275 mL
D. 2750 mL
Answer: D
2.75 L × 1000 mL/L = 2750 mL
