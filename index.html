How ChemUnlocked helps you learn general chemistry

Understand

Study guides that explain what is going on

Each unit starts with clear chemistry notes that explain the ideas behind the formulas. You are not just memorizing steps. You are learning what the problem is asking, why the method works, and what to notice before you start solving.
Practice

Practice problems that show what you actually know

The practice sets change each time, so you cannot rely on memory alone. Use them to check your understanding, find weak spots early, and see the full reasoning in the worked solutions. This is what students usually miss: getting one version right is not the same as being ready for the next version.
Study smart

Study skills that help chemistry stick

You also need a better way to study. These guides show you how to review, practice, and remember more effectively when chemistry starts piling up and rereading is no longer enough.
Practice Problems

Use practice to check your understanding before the test does

Choose a unit, work a short set, and then compare your thinking to the worked solution. Remember, the point is not just to see whether you were right. The point is to catch where your thinking went off track before that weak spot shows up on the test.
