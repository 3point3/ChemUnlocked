About These Guides

These articles draw on peer-reviewed learning science research and are written by an experienced high school science teacher. The goal isn't to tell you to "study harder" — it's to explain what cognitive science actually says about how memory works, and to give you concrete strategies that change how you prepare for any class, not just chemistry.
